Nina Barraco holds a BA in Art and Design from the Penny W. Stamps School of Art and Design at the University of Michigan, where she concentrated in graphic design and printmaking. Nina has always had an interest in interdisciplinary work and has garnered experience in a wide range of disciplines, from fine art to conservation to archaeology and art history. Her diverse background constantly informs her work as a designer and visual artist. Nina has worked as a graphic designer for a number of institutions in the Southeast Michigan region, including the University of Michigan and the Ann Arbor Summer Festival.
