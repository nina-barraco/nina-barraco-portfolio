Prioritizing the Needs of People & Communities

Client

Great Blue Orange Consulting

Role

UX/UI and development

The Challenge

Great Blue Orange Consulting, based in Traverse City, Michigan, was in need of a way to build their brand and connect with potential clients. In addition, they needed a platform where they could showcase their human-centric approach and proven track record of leading successful community initiatives.

My solution was the design and development of a website that would leverage their brand to highlight their services and unique approach through a few key methods: an easily accessible architecture, and an image-heavy design that depicts Northern Michigan and the community.
