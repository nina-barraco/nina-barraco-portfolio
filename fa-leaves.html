Leaf Abstractions

An exploration into form and abstraction through wood and ink.

In this series of prints, I explored the image of a leaf, and how I could take well-known object and abstract its form. Repetition and color were two methods of experimentation I implemented in particular. I desired to explore how we see things, and how something mundane or everyday could be transformed into something unusual. All of these prints are woodblock, with the first two printed on Hosho, and the rest on Okawara paper.
